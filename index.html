Solution-Focused Ministry is a Christian leadership book about a new mindset - being Solution-Focused - and about the new methods that flow from this biblical way of looking at life, ministry, people and problems.


The first three chapters detail the solution-focused mindset, and the last five chapters will look at how to apply this mindset to your ministry.


Solution-Focused Ministry is designed for all those who are in Christian service (pastors, evangelists, teachers, non-profit leaders and missionaries), to give them a path out of "being stuck". It combines scriptural insight with deep practical application and exercises that will help you to construct your own unique path into the future.

About the Author

John Edmiston is the CEO of Cybermissions, a Christain digital non-profit that provides free and low-cost theological educatio to those who need it most. John has lived in Australia, Nauru, Papua New Guinea, and the Philippines and he now lives in Virginia, USA with his wife Minda. John Edmiston has ministered in 18 nations.


John Edmiston was one of the early pioneers of internet ministry and mobile ministry. He has taught at the Asia Theological Seminary and Fuller Theological Seminary as well as at various bible schools and missionary conferences. John Edmiston is available to preach, teach, do podcasts, radio and TV shows, both in-person and by Zoom.
